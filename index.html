Tumor infiltrating lymphocytes (TIL) are part of the body's natural response to cancer. For patients who have not responded to or who have progressed on immune checkpoint inhibitors, TIL are being investigated as a potential treatment option in multiple solid tumors.1-3

IOVANCE Biotherapeutics is committed to deepening the understanding of TIL and optimizing TIL technology to help shape the next chapter of TIL cell therapy.
